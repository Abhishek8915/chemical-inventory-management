Help & Support

Getting Started

To get started with the Chemical Inventory System, follow these steps:

  • Log in to your account.
  • Navigate to the Inventory section to view and manage chemicals.
  • Use the search bar to find specific items.
  • Click show details button for detailed information.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new chemical?

To add a new chemical, go to the Inventory section and click on the "Add New Item" button. Fill in the required details and save.

How can I track chemical usage?

Chemical usage can be tracked through the Inventory section. You can view usage logs and generate reports from the Reports section.

What should I do if I encounter a problem?

If you encounter any issues, please contact our support team via the contact form on the Contact page or email us at support@chemicalinventory.com.
